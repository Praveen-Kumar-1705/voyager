Pro Tips

Travel Smarter

Insider knowledge for savvy Indian travellers

✈️

Book Flights Early

International flights from India are cheapest 8–12 weeks out. Domestic routes on IndiGo and Air India drop 30% when booked 3–4 weeks ahead.

🏨

Mix Accommodation

Splurge for 2 nights at a luxury property, then pick boutique stays. You get the wow moments without burning your entire budget.

💳

Zero-Fee Cards

Use HDFC Regalia, SBI Card Elite or Niyo Global for international travel — zero forex markup, free lounge access, and 5–10× reward points.

🗺️

Local Eats Win

Skip tourist menus — the best thali in Rajasthan or noodles in Bangkok is always one street away. Our AI builds local dining into every plan.

📱

SIM or eSIM First

Buy an Airtel or Jio international SIM at the airport or pick up a local eSIM via Airalo. Data is your lifeline — don't rely on hotel WiFi alone.

🛡️

Travel Insurance

Buy Bajaj Allianz or TATA AIG international travel insurance — it covers trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and baggage loss for under ₹2,000.

🧳

Pack Light, Live More

Carry-on only = no checked bag fees, no lost luggage, faster transit. Our AI generates climate-specific packing lists for every trip.

🌤️

Season Matters

Bali in July vs November is a completely different experience. Our AI automatically factors in weather, festivals, and peak-season pricing.

🎫

Pre-book Attractions

Taj Mahal, Louvre, Colosseum — book tickets 2–3 weeks in advance online to skip queues and save up to 20% on entry fees.
